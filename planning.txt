Artifact: Item that can be held and traded by players
    Rarity: subjective value of how rare this is
    Passive(): ability that happens passivley (gaining money, etc)
        -params: deltaT, player
    Active(): ability that happens on activation, optional cooldown
        -params: deltaT, player
    Cooldown: cooldown variable for active
    
Upgrade: Unremovable boost a player can unlock
